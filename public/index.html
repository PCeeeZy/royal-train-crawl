Party Like Royalty

A train crawl through the Northwest Chicago Burbs, dressed as royalty throughout history, competing to win a challenging scavenger hunt.
UP-NW Metra Line

The Union Pacific Northwest Metra Line will allow us to hit Palatine, Arlington Heights, Mt. Prospect, and Edison Park, conveniently and with access to bathrooms and ability to drink.

Wicked Royal

We will be dressed like we are royalty. Its going to be fun--its summertime and who doesn't like wearing a costume? Especially when it means you aren't a peasant. You aren't a peasant right?

Share Your Moments

I'm pretty cool, so I designed this website to allow y'all to submit media showcasing you completing your scavenger hunt. I'll turn it into a cool scrapbook or something. Let's go!
