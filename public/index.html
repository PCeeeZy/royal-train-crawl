Party Like Royalty

A train crawl through the Northwest Chicago Burbs, dressed as royalty throughout history, competing to win a challenging scavenger hunt.
UP-NW Metra Line

The Union Pacific Northwest Metra Line will allow us to hit Palatine, Arlington Heights, Mt. Prospect, and Edison Park, conveniently and with access to bathrooms and ability to drink.
