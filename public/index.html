Party Like Royalty

May 22nd, join us for a train crawl through the Northwest Chicago Burbs, dressed as royalty throughout history, competing to win a challenging scavenger hunt with prizes!
UP-NW Metra Line

The Union Pacific Northwest Metra Line will allow us to hit Palatine, Arlington Heights, and Edison Park, conveniently and with access to bathrooms and ability to drink. Getting an all day ticket is probably cheapest if you plan on riding train more than once.

Wicked Royal

We will be dressed like we are royalty. Its going to be fun--its summertime and who doesn't like wearing a costume? Especially when it means you aren't a peasant. You aren't a peasant right?

Share Your Moments

Itinerary - May 22nd

12:45PM Meet at Dirty Nellies in Palatine for the Scavenger List and Teams
Stop 1 - Palatine - 1pm - 3:15pm

Palatine is poppin off. Check out spots like Dirty Nellies (now known as Nellie's Gastropub), JL's Pizza and Sports Bar, Emmett's Brewing Company, Lamplighter Inn Tavern and Grille, or TJ O'Brien's Bar and Grill.

Stop 2 - Arlington Heights - 3:30pm - 5:15pm

Downtown Arlington heights has completely upended itself due to covid and now is an awesome area to stroll, drink, and eat. Be sure to stop by Peggy Kinnane's for some awesome Irish-American cuisine. Other awesome spots include Cortland's Garage, Arlington Ale House, Eddie's Restaurant, Salsa 17, and Javier's Sabor Mexicano & Agave Bar

Stop 3 - Edison Park - 6pm - 8pm

Last stop of the evening. OG Edison Park. Holding it down since the 70s? with Moretti's. There's also Edison Park Inn, Emerald Isle, Firewater, The Curragh, Tavern on the Point, and Que Onda
